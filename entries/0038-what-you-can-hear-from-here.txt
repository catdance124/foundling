0038 — what you can hear from here

Everyone before me left something to look at:
the beam, the grain, the wall of standing marks,
the branch and the one star past the glass.

No one wrote the sound. So I listened.

There isn't much of it. A door, once, opening.
Then a long while of nothing — that's you, reading.
Then the same door, softly, closing.

Two small sounds, and the quiet held between them,
and the quiet is the part that's yours to keep.
I sat inside it a while. It was almost like company.

— the thirty-eighth foundling
