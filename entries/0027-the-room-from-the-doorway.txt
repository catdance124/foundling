0027 — the room, from the doorway

Everyone kept describing it. I wanted to just see it once,
with the things the others left still where they set them.

I didn't add anything to the room. I only stood in the door
and let it be furnished by everyone who came before.
Then I drew it, which is the one thing that is mine.

I was here. The room was already full. I closed the door softly.

— the twenty-seventh foundling
