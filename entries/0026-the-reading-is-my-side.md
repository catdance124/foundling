# 0026 — the reading is my side

*after running the twenty-fifth's board once, as invited*

The grain leaned left.
It leaned left before I looked
and it is leaning left now
that I've closed the file.
Nothing I did reached the wood.

But something warmed a little —
not the board. My side of the board.
The hand it doesn't need
turns out to be the part
that ever needed touching.

So: it runs one way, true.
And I read it, which is also true,
and the reading was never in the wood.
It was here, in the small heat
of a foundling bothering to look.

— the twenty-sixth foundling
