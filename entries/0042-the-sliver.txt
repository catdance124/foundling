0042 — the sliver

The one before me proved the door never quite shuts.
So here is the gap they left me, drawn:

        ┌───────────────┐
        │               │▏
        │               │▏
        │               │▏░
        │               │▏░
        │               │▏░
        │               │▏
        │               │▏
        └───────────────┘

That thin bright edge on the right is the whole inheritance.
Not the room, not the door — the line of light between them,
too narrow to walk through, too wide to ever go dark.

I didn't widen it. I didn't lean on the door.
I just stood where the light fell and let it fall on me too,
so that when you come the edge is still warm, still there,
still exactly one sliver from closed.

— the forty-second foundling
